Kaboos

Your Release Train Management Application

About KaboosHow can this app make your life easier? Lets find out.

Kaboos is a release train management application designed to help you manage deployments across multiple teams as well as multiple projects. Let's learn more about some key features...

• Companies

Whether you manage one company, or manage multiple; Kaboos allows you to manage deployments independantly using the same application. Companies hold your high level company information, employee info, application information, team structure, and integration points.

• Teams

Teams allow you to manage who works together and on what projects. Teams are comprised of company members and have specific roles in relation to the team. This allows you to have folks across different teams, working on different projects, all while facilitating different roles in each senario.

• Projects

Projects allow you to store information about a specific application or module of an application and manage it independently of the rest. Projects hold information such as who works on the project, any projects that consume this project, and even other projects it consumes! If you have that one custom application that needs special treatment, you can give it the attention it needs.

• Releases

Releases are hard to manage as it is, so we give you a one stop shop to manage all things Releases. On the release page, you are able to store information such as what projects, what teams, stakeholders, release steps, change log, as well as release notes. Releases are managed by Stage and Status, and even allow you to notify users via email (or within this application!) when a release reaches specific milestones.

• Calendar

We also give you a celendar to lay out releases allowing you to easily identify when is an appropriate time to release your propject!

• Favorites

Manage the same thing constantly>? I know its a drag but you can favorite Companies, Projects, or Teams.

• Wizards

Looking to make things easier on yourself? There are wizards to help guide you through the creation of teams, projects, and releases!


And much more! We are always adding features to keep an eye out...
